About my Project 3

Jumprope

  • Pitch: My game is a jumprope challenge reflex game where you try to make your character jump over the incoming rope as many times as you can while it gets progressively faster.
  • Genre: Arcade
  • Platform: Desktop
  • Aesthetics: Simple, monochrome background and jumprope. The player character will be blue for visual draw and emphasis by standing out.
  • Gameplay: The players will be able to press the spacebar to make their character jump. If they do it at the right time they will clear the jumprope.
