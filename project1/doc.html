Original proposal: I plan to make my website about earthquakes. Some topics I wish to cover and plan to use/cut as space is used: basic knowledge of what earthquakes are and how they happen, the Richter Magnitude Scale, the historical 1989 Loma Prieta earthquake, the Great ShakeOut organization, and the Ring of Fire. The audience would be younger kids for classroom learning in middle school. I want to make the site's design simple and have a bit of color to it to attract and keep this viewer's attention.
