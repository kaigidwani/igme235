Starfield

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Bethesda Softworks LLC
  • Developed by: Bethesda Game Studios, Bethesda Game Studios Dallas LLC
  • Released: Sep 6, 2023

Description

Starfield is a singleplayer role-playing game set in space from the creators of the Elder Scrolls and Fallout. It is their first new IP in 25 years. In Starfield, you create your own character and tell their story with an adventure through the entire wide universe that you get to explore.

Game Categories

  • Genre: Role-playing (RPG)
  • Players: 1
  • Perspective: 1st-person, Behind view
  • Gameplay: Action RPG, Sandbox / Open world, Shooter
